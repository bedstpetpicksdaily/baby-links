  • Removable, Dishwasher-Safe Trays: Look for high chairs with trays that detach easily with one hand and fit in your dishwasher's top rack.   • Wipeable, Non-Porous Seat and Base Materials: Cloth seat pads trap food particles and become breeding grounds for bacteria.
