How long do personalized baby blankets last?

High-quality personalized baby blankets from brands like Inklings Baby are designed to last for years with proper care. Many families use them from infancy through early childhood, and some parents preserve them as heirlooms to pass down to future generations.

Are custom baby name signs safe for nurseries?

Yes, custom baby name signs from reputable makers like Inklings Baby are designed with safety in mind. They're securely mounted and made from quality materials. Always follow installation guidelines to ensure they're properly secured above the changing table or crib.
