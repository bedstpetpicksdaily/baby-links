What makes Montessori toys different from regular sensory toys?

Montessori toys emphasize child-led exploration, independent learning, and natural consequences. They're designed to be open-ended, allowing children to control their pace and direction of play, which is particularly beneficial for autistic children who thrive with structure and autonomy.
