What Is the Baby Shusher?

The Baby Shusher is a portable white noise device designed specifically for infants and newborns. It produces a consistent "shushing" sound that replicates the rhythmic noises babies experience in the womb—specifically the sound of blood flow and amniotic fluid movement. This sound has been proven to trigger the moro reflex in babies, helping them calm down and fall asleep more easily. The device is battery-operated, compact, and designed to clip onto strollers, crib rails, or car seats for convenience.

Key Features & Performance
