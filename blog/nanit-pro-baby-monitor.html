Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Nanit Pro work without WiFi?

No, the Nanit Pro requires a stable WiFi connection to function properly. It connects to your home network and allows remote viewing through the mobile app. Without WiFi, you won't be able to access live streaming or receive alerts.

Is the Nanit Pro camera safe for babies?

Yes, the Nanit Pro uses non-intrusive infrared night vision that doesn't emit harmful radiation. The camera is placed securely above the crib and poses no safety risks when installed according to manufacturer guidelines.

Can I use Nanit Pro with multiple children?

Yes, the Nanit Pro can monitor multiple rooms when you purchase additional camera units. Each camera connects to your account, allowing you to switch between feeds through the app.

What is Nanit's breathing detection feature?

The AI-powered breathing detection uses advanced algorithms to monitor your baby's breathing patterns without physical contact. It provides insights into sleep quality and can alert you to unusual breathing patterns for added peace of mind.
