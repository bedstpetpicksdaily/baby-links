Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Cocoon actually fit against any bed frame?

Mostly, but not universally. It works best with platform beds and standard bed frames. If you have a sleigh bed, ornate headboard, or adjustable base, you'll need to test the fit first. The locking arms have limits on how much they adjust—roughly 2-3 inches of variation. Platform beds and IKEA frames are typically fine; Victorian furniture and adjustable Sleep Number beds often create fitment issues.

How portable is this really if I want to travel?

Portable-ish. The modular design means you can detach the bassinet portion and carry it, which beats hauling an entire Pack and Play. However, you're still dealing with a fairly bulky item that requires a carrying bag (often sold separately) and doesn't pack down smaller than a standard diaper bag. Good for moving between your bedroom and guest room; impractical for week-long vacations where luggage is tight.

What's the weight limit and when do babies outgrow it?

Most babies hit the 15-20 pound weight limit around 5-7 months, though it varies based on baby size. Some larger newborns will max it out closer to 6 months. This is shorter than a traditional crib (which handles up to 35+ pounds), so you'll need a separate sleeping solution ready before that happens. Plan accordingly—don't buy this as your only sleeping surface.
