Frequently Asked Questions

What age range is the Owlet Smart Sock 3 designed for?

The Owlet Smart Sock 3 is designed for infants from birth through 18 months old. The sock comes in different sizes to accommodate your baby's growth throughout this period.

How long does the Owlet sock battery last?

The battery typically lasts 18+ hours on a single charge, depending on usage. Most parents charge the sock overnight while their baby wears it, ensuring continuous monitoring during sleep.

Does Owlet Smart Sock 3 work without WiFi?

The sock requires a WiFi connection at home for initial setup and monitoring. The base station connects to your network, though you can view alerts on your phone even when away from home.

Is the Owlet Smart Sock 3 medically approved?

The Owlet Smart Sock 3 is FDA-cleared as a general wellness device. It's designed to monitor heart rate and oxygen levels but should not replace professional medical advice from your pediatrician.

Can multiple family members receive alerts?

Yes, the Owlet app allows you to invite multiple caregivers and family members who can receive real-time notifications and view your baby's data on their own devices.
