Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to prepare a bottle from start to finish?

From pressing the button to having a ready-to-drink bottle takes approximately 30-45 seconds, depending on how much formula you're mixing. This includes heating the water, dispensing it, and adding formula through the machine's integrated funnel. In comparison, manually heating water in a kettle typically takes 5-7 minutes, then another 3-5 minutes cooling it down to safe drinking temperature.

Can you use this machine with all formula brands?

Yes, the Tommee Tippee Perfect Prep works with all standard powdered infant formulas. The machine measures and dispenses the correct water-to-powder ratio automatically, so switching between brands (like going from Similac to Enfamil) isn't a problem. However, it's not designed for ready-to-feed liquid formula or specialized thick formulas, so check your specific formula type first.

Is the water quality safe for newborns, and how often does it need cleaning?

The machine includes a built-in water filter, and the water is heated to 70°C, which is hot enough to kill most pathogens according to NHS guidelines. Descaling should be done monthly using the recommended solution (usually white vinegar works), and the water tank should be emptied and rinsed daily if left sitting. I descale mine every 4 weeks and haven't experienced any mineral buildup or water quality issues.
