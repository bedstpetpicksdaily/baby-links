What age are Joyrealtoys products suitable for?

Are Joyrealtoys products safe for children who mouth toys?

Yes, Joyrealtoys prioritizes safety with non-toxic materials and thoughtful design. Always review specific product safety information, especially for younger children or those who engage in mouthing behaviors.

Can AAC devices and toys help with speech development?

AAC devices support communication development by giving children a voice, reducing frustration, and often naturally encouraging language growth. They're not meant to replace speech therapy but complement it as part of a holistic communication approach.

How do Montessori toys differ from regular sensory toys?

Montessori toys emphasize child-led discovery, independent learning, and purposeful skill-building. They typically have fewer distractions and clearer developmental goals compared to traditional sensory toys focused solely on stimulation.
