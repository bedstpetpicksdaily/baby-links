Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Babymoov Aloona compare to fixed-position monitors?

The pan-tilt-zoom gives you flexibility that fixed cameras can't match—you can follow your baby from one corner of the crib to another without manually repositioning the unit. That said, if your nursery is small and your camera placement is central, a fixed monitor with excellent optics might be sufficient. The mechanical advantage comes at the cost of more moving parts that could theoretically fail, though the Aloona's durability seems solid based on the 500+ reviews.

Is the WiFi connection reliable enough to trust for checking in while you're away?

Generally yes—once properly paired, most users report stable connections. However, don't expect instant loading every single time. There's occasionally a 2-5 second delay, and the app sometimes needs a manual refresh if your phone has been idle. For peace of mind checking, this is acceptable. For real-time emergency response, consider whether a dedicated closed-circuit option might serve you better.

What's included in the base price, and what costs extra?

The base purchase includes the camera, power adapter, mounting hardware, and access to local streaming via the app. Cloud storage for video clips, advanced motion alerts, and extended video history require a subscription tier. If you only use local WiFi streaming and don't need cloud backup, you avoid those recurring fees entirely—worth factoring into your total cost of ownership.
