Why Bamboo Baby Pajamas Are Ideal for Summer

Bamboo fabric is a game-changer for warm-weather baby sleepwear. Unlike traditional cotton, bamboo viscose is incredibly breathable, moisture-wicking, and naturally hypoallergenic. This means your baby stays cool and dry throughout the night, reducing the risk of heat rash and sleep disruptions during hot summer months. The fabric also has natural thermoregulating properties, keeping your baby comfortable in fluctuating temperatures.
