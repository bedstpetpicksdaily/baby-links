What is an Infant Travel System?

An infant travel system is a convenient combination of a car seat and stroller frame that work together seamlessly. This allows you to click your car seat directly into the stroller base without disturbing your sleeping baby when transitioning from car to stroller. Most quality travel systems are designed for infants from birth up to 30 pounds, making them an essential purchase for new parents who spend a lot of time on the go.

Baby Trend offers several excellent travel system options that combine safety, functionality, and affordability. Their systems are engineered to work with LATCH connectors and typically include both the infant car seat and matching stroller frame.

Many Baby Trend car seats are compatible with other stroller brands that feature standard infant car seat adapters. However, they work best with Baby Trend's matching stroller frames for optimal fit and functionality.
