Yes, the movie is based on the true and tragic 2019 case of newborn Heidi Broussard who was kidnapped from a Texas hospital by Megan Fieramusca, a woman who had faked her own pregnancy. The case received national attention and was solved within days when authorities located the baby safe.

The movie is typically rated TV-14 for dramatic tension, emotional intensity, and some potentially disturbing subject matter involving infant abduction. Parents should be aware it deals with serious crimes and may be distressing for sensitive viewers.

Yes, baby Heidi is found safe and returned to her mother Broussard after just a few days. However, the film still addresses the trauma and emotional impact of the kidnapping on the family.
