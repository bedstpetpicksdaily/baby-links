Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Montessori toys different from regular sensory toys?

Montessori toys emphasize self-directed learning and natural development stages. They minimize distractions, focus on one or two learning concepts per toy, and encourage problem-solving. Joyrealtoys applies these principles specifically for children with autism, creating toys that support focus and reduce sensory overwhelm.
