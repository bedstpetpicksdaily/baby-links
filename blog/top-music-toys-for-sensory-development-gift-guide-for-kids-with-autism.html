Why Music Toys Matter for Sensory Development

Music toys play a crucial role in sensory integration for children with autism. They provide structured auditory input, encourage cause-and-effect learning, and can serve as a bridge to communication and social interaction. Quality music toys help develop fine and gross motor skills, hand-eye coordination, and cognitive abilities while keeping children engaged and motivated to explore and play.

Key Features to Look for in Autism-Friendly Music Toys
