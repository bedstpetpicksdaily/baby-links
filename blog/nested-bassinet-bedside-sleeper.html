Frequently Asked Questions

Is a bedside bassinet safe for co-sleeping?

Yes, when used correctly according to manufacturer guidelines. Bedside bassinets create a separate sleep surface while keeping your baby nearby, following safe sleep recommendations from pediatric organizations.

How long can a baby use a nested bassinet?

Most babies outgrow bassinets between 4-6 months when they begin rolling or reaching the weight limit, typically 15-20 pounds depending on the model.

Can a bedside bassinet fit on any bed?

No, compatibility varies by model. Check your bed frame height, mattress thickness, and whether your bed has a footboard before purchasing to ensure proper attachment.

What features should I look for in a quality bassinet?

Prioritize firm, breathable mattresses, secure attachment mechanisms, easy height adjustment, stable construction, and compliance with CPSC safety standards.
