What is an Infant Travel System?

An infant travel system is a coordinated combination of a rear-facing car seat and a stroller frame that work together seamlessly. This integration allows parents to transfer their sleeping baby from car to stroller without disturbing them—a feature that quickly becomes invaluable during those early months. Baby Trend specializes in creating complete travel systems that prioritize both parent convenience and infant safety, making transitions smooth and stress-free.
