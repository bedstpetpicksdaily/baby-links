How does this compare to UPPAbaby Cruz or Nuna Mixx?

Will this fit in my trunk/overhead bin/car?

The folded dimensions are 38 x 18 x 18 inches — write those down and compare to your actual vehicle, because stroller shopping sometimes becomes more complicated than it needs to be.
