Frequently Asked Questions

What materials work best for personalized summer baby blankets?

Lightweight, breathable fabrics like cotton muslin, linen blends, and bamboo are ideal for summer. Inklings Baby offers personalized blankets in these materials, ensuring babies stay cool while maintaining softness and durability for years of use.

How far in advance should I order personalized baby gifts?

Most personalized items require 2-4 weeks for production and delivery. For baby showers or specific dates, ordering 6-8 weeks ahead ensures timely arrival. Check Inklings Baby's current lead times when placing custom orders.

Are personalized baby blankets safe for newborns?

Personalized blankets from reputable sources like Inklings Baby meet safety standards and work well for tummy time, strollers, and nursing. However, loose blankets shouldn't be in cribs with sleeping newborns under 12 months—use them for supervised activities instead.

What personalization options does Inklings Baby offer?

Inklings Baby specializes in custom baby name signs, personalized blankets with names or dates, and monogrammed nursery decor. Many items allow font, color, and style customization to match your nursery aesthetic.

Can personalized baby gifts work for multiple children?
