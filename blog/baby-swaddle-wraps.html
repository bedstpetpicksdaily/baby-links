Frequently Asked Questions

What age can babies use swaddle wraps?

Most swaddle wraps are suitable for newborns from birth up to 3-4 months. Always check the manufacturer's recommendations for size and weight limits.

Are swaddle wraps safe for babies?

Yes, when used correctly, swaddle wraps are safe and beneficial. Ensure the wrap is snug but not too tight, and always place baby on their back to sleep.

What's the difference between velcro and traditional swaddles?

Velcro wraps are faster and easier for parents to use, while traditional wraps offer more adjustability. Both are equally safe when used properly.

Can swaddle wraps help with reflux?

Swaddling can provide comfort to babies with mild reflux by creating a secure environment, though it's not a treatment. Consult your pediatrician for specific concerns.
