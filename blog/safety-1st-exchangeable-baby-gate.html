Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Safety 1st Exchangeable Baby Gate suitable for stairs?

Yes, it can be used at the top or bottom of stairs, though some pressure-mounted gates require the optional hardware kit for stair installation. Always verify compatibility and consider your specific staircase configuration.

What doorway widths does this gate accommodate?

The Safety 1st Exchangeable Baby Gate typically fits doorways from 24.5 to 42 inches wide. The exchangeable panels allow for customization within this range, and extension kits are available for wider openings.

How do I install the pressure-mounted baby gate?

Installation involves positioning the gate in the doorway, extending the top and bottom pressure bars against the frame, and securing them firmly. No tools or wall damage required. Follow manufacturer instructions carefully for optimal safety.

Can I leave this gate up permanently?

While the gate is designed for long-term use, the pressure bars may loosen over time if left extended. Regular tension checks are recommended to maintain safety and prevent gate failure.
