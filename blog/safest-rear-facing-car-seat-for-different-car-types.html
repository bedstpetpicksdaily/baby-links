What to Look For

  • Installation flexibility: Look for car seats offering both LATCH and seat belt installation options. Choose seats with clearly marked recline angle guides or automatic recline adjustment features—these are especially important in vehicles with sloped seat backs (like sports cars or certain crossovers) where achieving the correct angle matters more.
