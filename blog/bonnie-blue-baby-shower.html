Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Bonnie Blue baby shower theme?

Bonnie Blue is a soft, muted blue color that works perfectly for baby showers, offering a calming and sophisticated aesthetic. This theme typically features light blue decorations, soft pastels, and elegant accents that appeal to parents who want something gentle and timeless rather than bright primary colors. It's popular for both boys' showers and gender-neutral celebrations.

What decorations do I need for a Bonnie Blue baby shower?

Start with Bonnie Blue balloons, streamers, and tablecloths as your base, then add white or cream-colored accents and soft green plants for contrast. Consider incorporating wood elements, fairy lights, or delicate floral arrangements to enhance the soft, serene aesthetic. You can also use pale gray, ivory, or champagne metallic accents to elevate the look without overwhelming the soft blue color.

What food and cake ideas work for a Bonnie Blue shower?

A white or cream-colored cake with Bonnie Blue fondant details, macarons, or edible flowers creates a cohesive look without being overwhelming. Serve light refreshments like sugar cookies decorated in blue and white, blue velvet cupcakes, or pastries dusted with edible pearl dust. Blue-tinted beverages like lemonade or punch (with berries for color) fit the theme while keeping the focus subtle and elegant.

Can I do a Bonnie Blue baby shower for a girl?

Absolutely—Bonnie Blue works beautifully for girls' showers and is increasingly popular as a gender-neutral or modern alternative to traditional pink. Pair it with soft pinks, rose gold accents, or white florals to add femininity while keeping the sophisticated, muted aesthetic. Many parents appreciate Bonnie Blue for girls because it feels elegant and timeless rather than overly sweet or trendy.
