Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Nuna Rava compare to the Britax Boulevard and Chicco Nextfit?

All three are solid ISOFIX convertible seats in similar price ranges. The Nuna Rava edges ahead in recline smoothness and fabric durability based on real-world testing. The Britax Boulevard offers slightly easier harness adjustments, while the Chicco Nextfit is lighter if vehicle-switching is your priority. The Nuna's 4.3-star rating reflects strong middle-ground performance rather than excelling in any single category.

Can the Nuna Rava be used with just the seat belt if I don't have ISOFIX?

Yes, absolutely. ISOFIX is the premium installation method, but the seat belt installation works perfectly fine. However, if you're choosing between this seat and a belt-only alternative at the same price, the ISOFIX option gives you more flexibility for future vehicles. Many families install with ISOFIX in their primary vehicle and keep the belt option as backup.

Is July a good time to buy a convertible car seat, or should I wait for fall sales?

July is actually ideal if you're planning summer road trips or second-car installations before school year starts. Car seat sales patterns typically dip mid-summer, so prices are often competitive. Black Friday brings steeper discounts, but if you need a seat now for upcoming travel, July purchasing won't cost significantly more than waiting three months.
