What does 4.8 out of 5 stars really mean for a product?

A 4.8 out of 5 stars rating means the product has excellent customer satisfaction with very few complaints. This rating typically indicates that out of 100 reviews, roughly 80-90% gave it 5 stars, with the remaining being 4-star reviews, showing it's a reliable choice for most buyers.

Is 4.8 stars good enough or should I wait for a 5 star product?

A 4.8 rating is excellent and often more trustworthy than a perfect 5 stars, which can indicate fake reviews or limited feedback. No product is perfect for everyone, so a 4.8 with thousands of reviews is typically a safer purchase than a 5.0 with only a few reviews.

Should I read the 1 and 2 star reviews if a product has 4.8 stars?

Yes, you should definitely check the lower-rated reviews to understand specific issues like durability, sizing, or quality concerns. These reviews often reveal important details about whether the product's flaws matter for your specific needs and use case.
