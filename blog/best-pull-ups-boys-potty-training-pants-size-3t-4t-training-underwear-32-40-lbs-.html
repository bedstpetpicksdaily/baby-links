    What size pull ups should I buy for my 3T 4T toddler?

    How many pull ups do I need per day for potty training?

    Most toddlers need 8-12 pull ups daily depending on fluid intake and training stage—typically 1-2 for naps, 1-2 for nighttime, and the rest for daytime accidents.     Pull ups offer convenience and leak protection, making them ideal for on-the-go, while training underwear helps children feel wet sensations better, encouraging faster learning.
