What is the age requirement for babies at Electric Forest 2026?

Electric Forest allows babies of all ages to attend the festival with a parent or guardian. However, infants under 2 years old do not require a ticket, though they will need one starting at age 2. Always check the official Electric Forest website closer to the event for any updates to their infant policy.

Is Electric Forest 2026 safe for babies and young children?

Electric Forest has designated family-friendly areas and quieter zones away from the main stages, making it more manageable for parents with babies. The festival provides medical tents and support services, but keep in mind the outdoor festival environment with crowds, noise, and varying weather can be challenging for infants. Many parents recommend waiting until children are at least 3-5 years old before bringing them to the full festival experience.

What should I bring for my baby at Electric Forest?

Pack a stroller or baby carrier for easy mobility through crowds, plenty of diapers, wipes, and baby food or formula. Bring sun protection like a hat and sunscreen, a lightweight blanket or shade cover, and ensure you have access to clean water and a quiet space for naps. Don't forget a small first-aid kit and any medications your baby might need.

Can I bring a stroller to Electric Forest 2026?

Yes, strollers are allowed at Electric Forest and highly recommended for navigating the campgrounds and festival grounds with a baby. Lightweight or all-terrain strollers work best on the festival's terrain, and consider a stroller wagon if you're bringing multiple small children. Be prepared that some areas may get crowded, so a baby carrier as a backup option is also helpful.
