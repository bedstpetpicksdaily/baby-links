Frequently Asked Questions

What are bamboo viscose swaddles best used for?

Bamboo viscose swaddles are perfect for swaddling newborns, nursing covers, stroller blankets, tummy time mats, and diaper changes. Their versatility makes them an essential multi-purpose item in any nursery.

Are bamboo viscose swaddles safe for sensitive baby skin?

Yes, bamboo viscose is naturally hypoallergenic, dermatologist-tested, and gentle on sensitive skin. It's free from harsh chemicals, making it ideal for newborns prone to eczema or allergies.

How do I care for bamboo viscose baby swaddles?

Wash swaddles in cold water on a gentle cycle with mild detergent. Air dry or tumble dry on low heat to maintain softness and longevity. Avoid fabric softeners and bleach.

What size swaddle should I choose for my baby?

Most quality bamboo viscose swaddles measure 47x47 inches, providing ample coverage for newborns through toddlers. This size offers flexibility for various swaddling techniques and uses.
