Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it typically take to receive a personalized baby name sign?

Most custom baby name signs are made-to-order, typically taking 2-4 weeks for production and delivery. However, timeframes vary by provider. If you need your sign before baby arrives, order as early as possible. Check with your chosen brand, like Inklings Baby, for their specific turnaround times.

Are personalized baby name signs safe to hang above a crib?

Safety is paramount in nurseries. Ensure any sign you choose is securely mounted with appropriate hardware and is lightweight enough that it cannot pose a falling hazard. Brands like Inklings Baby design their personalized baby gifts with nursery safety in mind. Always follow installation instructions carefully and avoid placing heavy items directly above your baby's sleep area.

Can I change the name on a personalized baby name sign if I haven't finalized my baby's name?

Most custom baby name signs cannot be easily altered once created, as they're made-to-order with permanent lettering. It's best to finalize your baby's name before ordering. Some providers may offer remaking options, but this typically involves additional costs. If you're unsure, consider waiting until you've decided on a name.

What's the best way to care for and maintain a personalized baby name sign?

Care depends on the material. Wooden signs should be dusted regularly and kept away from excessive moisture. Acrylic and metal signs can be gently wiped clean. Avoid direct sunlight to prevent fading. Follow any specific care instructions provided by your sign's manufacturer to keep it looking beautiful for years to come.
