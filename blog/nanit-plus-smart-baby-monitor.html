Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Nanit Plus work without WiFi?

The Nanit Plus requires a stable WiFi connection for remote viewing and full functionality. Local viewing on the same network may work with degraded connectivity, but we recommend a strong WiFi signal for optimal performance.

Is the Nanit Plus compatible with other smart home devices?

Yes, the Nanit Plus integrates with popular smart home platforms including Alexa and Google Home, allowing you to control features and view your baby monitor through your existing smart home ecosystem.

What makes the sleep tracking feature unique?

The AI-powered sleep tracking uses advanced algorithms to monitor your baby's sleep cycles, movement patterns, and duration without any wearables. This technology provides actionable insights to help improve sleep quality.
