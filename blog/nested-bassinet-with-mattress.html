Frequently Asked Questions

What age can babies use a nested bassinet?

Nested bassinets are designed for newborns from birth until approximately 4-6 months old, or until baby begins to roll over or sit up independently.

Is a bassinet mattress necessary or can I use my own?

Using the included bassinet mattress is recommended for proper fit and safety. Custom mattresses may not fit securely and could pose hazards.

How do I attach a nested bassinet to my bed?

Most models use adjustable straps or clips that secure to your bed frame. Check compatibility with your specific bed type before purchasing.

Are nested bassinets safe for co-sleeping?

Yes, when used correctly with proper mattress firmness and no loose bedding, nested bassinets provide a safe designated sleep space while keeping baby close.
