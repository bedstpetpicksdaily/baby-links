What Are Soothing Devices for Newborns?

Soothing devices are tools designed to help calm babies and promote better sleep by producing consistent, gentle sounds that create a calming environment. These devices work by mimicking sounds babies are familiar with from their time in the womb—particularly the constant background noise of amniotic fluid and the mother's heartbeat. Unlike traditional white noise machines, the best soothing devices produce specific frequencies and tones that are scientifically proven to settle fussy babies and help them drift into deeper, longer sleep cycles.
