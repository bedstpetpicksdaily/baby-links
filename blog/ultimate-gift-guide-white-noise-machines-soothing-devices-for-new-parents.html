Why White Noise & Soothing Devices Matter for Newborns

Newborns spend nine months in the womb, a surprisingly noisy environment filled with constant ambient sounds. When babies enter the world, silence can actually be startling and disruptive to their sleep. White noise machines and soothing devices recreate this familiar soundscape, helping babies feel secure and comfortable. Research shows that consistent, calming sounds can reduce stress hormones, improve sleep quality, and even help with colic. Parents report that introducing these devices leads to longer nap times, earlier bedtimes, and fewer nighttime wakings—meaning more rest for everyone.
