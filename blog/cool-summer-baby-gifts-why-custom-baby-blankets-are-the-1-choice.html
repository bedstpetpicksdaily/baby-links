Frequently Asked Questions

What size custom baby blanket is best for summer use?

A 30x40 inch personalized baby blanket is ideal for summer, offering versatility for strollers, car seats, and cribs while remaining lightweight and portable. This size is substantial enough to be functional yet easy to carry during outings.

Can I wash a personalized baby blanket in hot water?

Most quality custom baby blankets, including those from Inklings Baby, should be washed in warm water and tumble dried on low heat to preserve both the personalization and fabric quality. Always check the care label for specific recommendations based on your blanket's material.
