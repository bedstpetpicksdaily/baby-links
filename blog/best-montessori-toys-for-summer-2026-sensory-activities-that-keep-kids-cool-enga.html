What Makes Montessori Toys Perfect for Summer Play?

Montessori toys are designed around the principle of child-led learning. They encourage kids to explore their environment, discover cause-and-effect relationships, and develop independence through play. During summer, when children have more free time, these toys provide meaningful engagement without excessive screen time. Water play toys, sensory bins, and tactile activities are especially popular during warmer months, offering both cooling relief and developmental benefits.
