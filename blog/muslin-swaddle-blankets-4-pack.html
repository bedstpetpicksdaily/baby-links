Frequently Asked Questions

What size are muslin swaddle blankets?

Most muslin swaddle blankets measure 47x47 inches, providing ample coverage for swaddling newborns and versatile use as your baby grows.

Are muslin blankets safe for babies?

Yes, muslin blankets are safe for babies. The breathable fabric reduces overheating risk, making them ideal for swaddling. Always follow safe sleep guidelines and avoid loose blankets in the crib.

How many muslin blankets do I need?

A 4-pack is ideal for most families, providing enough blankets for daily use while others are being washed. You may want additional sets if doing laundry less frequently.

How do I care for muslin swaddle blankets?

Wash in warm water with gentle detergent and tumble dry on low heat. Avoid bleach and fabric softeners to maintain softness and breathability over time.
