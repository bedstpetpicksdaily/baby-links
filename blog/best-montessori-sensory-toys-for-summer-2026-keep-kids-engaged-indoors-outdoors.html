Can sensory toys be used both indoors and outdoors during summer?

How do AAC communication devices from Joyrealtoys support play?

What makes Montessori toys different from regular sensory toys?

Montessori toys follow child-led learning principles with open-ended use, natural materials, and self-correcting features. They promote independence, critical thinking, and intrinsic motivation—Joyrealtoys specifically honors these principles in their design philosophy.
