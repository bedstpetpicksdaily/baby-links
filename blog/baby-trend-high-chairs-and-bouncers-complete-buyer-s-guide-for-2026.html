Frequently Asked Questions

What age can babies use Baby Trend high chairs?

Most Baby Trend high chairs are designed for babies starting around 6 months when they can sit upright independently. Always check the specific model's age and weight recommendations before purchase to ensure proper fit and safety.

Are Baby Trend bouncers safe for newborns?

Yes, Baby Trend bouncers are designed with newborn safety features including proper head support and secure harness systems. Many models accommodate infants from birth, though specific age guidelines vary by model. Always verify the product specifications match your baby's age and weight.

How do I clean Baby Trend high chair trays?

Most Baby Trend high chair trays are removable and dishwasher-safe, making cleanup incredibly easy. Simply detach the tray and place it in the dishwasher, or hand wash with warm soapy water. The chair frame can be wiped down with a damp cloth.

Do Baby Trend bouncers fold up for storage?

Many Baby Trend bouncer models feature compact folding designs that make storage convenient. However, not all models fold, so check product specifications if portability and storage are priorities for your household.
