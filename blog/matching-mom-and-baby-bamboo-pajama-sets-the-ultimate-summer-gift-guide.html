Why Bamboo Pajamas Are Perfect for Summer

Bamboo fabric has revolutionized baby sleepwear for good reason. During summer months, babies can overheat easily, leading to disrupted sleep and discomfort. Bamboo's natural temperature-regulating properties keep little ones cool while preventing night sweats.
