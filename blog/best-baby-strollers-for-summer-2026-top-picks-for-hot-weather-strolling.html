What makes a stroller suitable for hot weather?

Summer strollers need breathable fabrics, expanded sun canopies with UPF protection, lightweight frames that don't absorb heat, and excellent ventilation through mesh panels. These features work together to keep babies cool while protecting them from sun exposure.

Are Baby Trend strollers compatible with car seats?

Yes, Baby Trend offers comprehensive infant travel systems that seamlessly integrate their strollers with compatible car seats, providing convenient transitions from vehicle to stroller without disturbing your sleeping baby.

How often should I clean summer stroller fabrics?

During summer months when your stroller gets heavy use, clean fabrics every 1-2 weeks using mild soap and water. Most Baby Trend models feature quick-dry, washable seat liners designed for easy maintenance and hygiene.
