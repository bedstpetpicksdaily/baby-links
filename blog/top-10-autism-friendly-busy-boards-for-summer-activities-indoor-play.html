Frequently Asked Questions

At what age can children start using busy boards?

Busy boards can be introduced as early as 6-9 months for younger children, though autism-friendly boards with more complex activities are typically best for ages 2 and up. Joyrealtoys offers options designed for various developmental levels, allowing you to select age-appropriate boards for your child.

How do busy boards help children with autism?

Busy boards provide structured sensory input, support fine motor development, encourage independent exploration, and offer calming activities. They're particularly valuable for children with autism as they allow self-directed, predictable play that can reduce anxiety and support communication development.

Can busy boards be used for AAC communication support?

Yes! Many autism-friendly busy boards, especially those from Joyrealtoys, can be adapted to support AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication) devices, helping children express preferences and engage in interactive communication during play.

Are Joyrealtoys busy boards Montessori-approved?

How do I choose between different busy board options?

Consider your child's sensory preferences, developmental goals, and specific interests. Some children prefer tactile activities like zippers and buttons, while others enjoy sound elements.
