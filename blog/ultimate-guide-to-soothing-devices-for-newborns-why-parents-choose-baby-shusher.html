Understanding Newborn Sleep Challenges

Newborns have unpredictable sleep patterns, waking frequently due to hunger, discomfort, or overstimulation. Unlike adults, babies haven't developed self-soothing skills and rely entirely on external comfort methods. Environmental factors like sudden noises, light changes, and temperature fluctuations can jolt them awake, disrupting crucial sleep cycles. Sleep deprivation affects not only the baby's development but also parental well-being and family dynamics.

How White Noise Soothes Newborns
