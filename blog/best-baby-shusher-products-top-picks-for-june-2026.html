Yes, Baby Shusher devices are designed for extended use and are safe for all-night operation. However, it's recommended to set the volume to a safe level (below 50-60 decibels) to protect your baby's hearing. Many models include automatic shut-off timers for convenience.

Can Baby Shusher products be used from birth?

Most Baby Shusher products are safe for newborns from birth. However, it's best to place the device at a safe distance from the crib and avoid direct sound exposure. Always follow the manufacturer's guidelines for your specific model.
