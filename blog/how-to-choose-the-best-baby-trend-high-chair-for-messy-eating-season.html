Frequently Asked Questions

How do I clean a Baby Trend high chair after messy meals?

Most Baby Trend high chairs feature removable, dishwasher-safe trays and wipeable upholstery. Simply wipe down the seat and frame with a damp cloth, remove the tray for washing, and if cushions are removable, toss them in the washer. For stubborn spots, use mild soap and warm water.

What age can babies start using a Baby Trend high chair?

Most Baby Trend high chairs are suitable for babies 6 months and older who can sit upright with minimal support. Always check the specific model's guidelines, as some offer recline positions for younger babies transitioning to solids.

Are Baby Trend high chairs safe for my baby?

Yes, Baby Trend high chairs meet or exceed CPSC safety standards and feature secure harness systems, stable bases, and thorough safety testing. Always use the five-point harness and ensure proper assembly for maximum safety.

Do Baby Trend high chairs take up a lot of space?

Baby Trend offers both full-size and compact models. Compact versions are designed for smaller kitchens, and some fold for storage. Measure your space before purchasing and consider which model best fits your home layout.
