Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Nanobébé compare to buying a separate warmer and sterilizer?

Financially, two separate devices might cost less individually, but combined they occupy nearly double the counter or cabinet space. The Nanobébé's real value lies in consolidation and workflow efficiency—no switching between appliances. For parents managing multiple bottles daily, the time saved justifies the premium. If you're price-sensitive and have storage space, two budget devices will technically work, but they won't integrate as cleanly into your routine.

Does it actually preserve breast milk nutrients during warming?

Yes, notably better than traditional bottle warmers. The Nanobébé uses a gentler heating method that avoids hot spots that can damage milk proteins and beneficial antibodies. Breast milk heated too aggressively loses nutritional value, and this device maintains lower, more consistent temperatures throughout the warming cycle. This matters if you're exclusively pumping and want maximum nutrient retention.

Is the sterilization cycle actually effective against common bacteria?

The steam sterilization cycle reaches temperatures sufficient to eliminate 99.9% of harmful bacteria and viruses when used properly. Complete the full cycle without interrupting it, and results match hospital-grade sterilization. However, if you're using it multiple times daily during peak summer, mineral buildup from water becomes an issue—use distilled water every third cycle to maintain effectiveness and prevent clogging.
