Frequently Asked Questions

How does the DuoGlider compare to side-by-side double strollers?

Tandem strollers like the DuoGlider win on width and maneuverability in tight spaces, but the rear seat does sacrifice some visibility and direct parent interaction. Side-by-side models offer equal positioning but are significantly wider and harder to navigate through standard doorways. Choose tandem if you value portability; choose side-by-side if both kids demand premium seating positions.

Can you fit a car seat in the bottom seat?

The DuoGlider is compatible with Graco infant car seats via Click Connect adapters, allowing you to snap a carrier into the lower position without removing the full seat. This is genuinely convenient for newborns, though you'll need to verify specific car seat model compatibility before purchasing.

Is assembly difficult for someone without mechanical skills?

Assembly takes roughly 20-30 minutes and requires only a Phillips screwdriver—no special tools needed. The instructions are reasonably clear, and most first-time stroller owners complete setup without major frustration. Some users report minor wheel-alignment tweaks needed post-assembly, but nothing that requires professional help.
