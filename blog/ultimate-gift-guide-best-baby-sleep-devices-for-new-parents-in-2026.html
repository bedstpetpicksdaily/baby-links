Creating the Ideal Sleep Environment

The most effective approach combines a quality sleep device with proper nursery setup. Maintain a cool room temperature (68-72°F), use blackout curtains, and ensure minimal light exposure. Layer your sleep aids: use white noise or shushing sounds as the foundation, add gentle vibrations if your device supports them, and consider swaddling for newborns. Consistency is key—establishing a nightly routine with your chosen sleep device helps babies develop sleep associations that last for months.
