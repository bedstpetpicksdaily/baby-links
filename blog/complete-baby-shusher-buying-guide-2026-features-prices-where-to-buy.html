Frequently Asked Questions

At what age can babies use Baby Shusher?

Baby Shusher is safe and effective for newborns from birth onwards. Many parents introduce it in the hospital or within the first few weeks at home. Always ensure the device is placed at a safe distance and at appropriate volume levels for your baby's age.

How loud should the Baby Shusher be set?

The optimal volume should be similar to the level of a running shower or vacuum cleaner—approximately 50-60 decibels. Start at a lower volume and gradually increase until you find your baby's sweet spot. Avoid setting it too loud to prevent hearing damage.

Can Baby Shusher be used during daytime naps?

Absolutely! Many parents use Baby Shusher for both nighttime sleep and daytime naps. Consistent use of the shushing sound throughout the day and night can establish better sleep associations and improve overall sleep quality.

Is Baby Shusher safe for overnight use?

Yes, Baby Shusher is designed for safe overnight use. However, ensure the device is placed outside the crib and positioned away from your baby's face. If using a battery-powered model, check battery levels before bedtime.
