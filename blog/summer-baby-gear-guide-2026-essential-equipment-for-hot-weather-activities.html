How do I keep my baby cool in a stroller during summer?

Choose strollers with breathable mesh panels, extendable canopies, and ventilation systems. Baby Trend strollers feature these cooling technologies plus reflective materials that minimize heat absorption from the sun.

Are Baby Trend car seats safe for hot weather?

Yes, Baby Trend car seats meet all safety standards and feature heat-resistant materials and ventilation design. Always use a sunshade when the car is parked, and test the seat surface before placing your baby in it during extremely hot weather.
