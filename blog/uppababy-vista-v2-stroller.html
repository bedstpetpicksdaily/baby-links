Frequently Asked Questions

What age can a newborn use the UPPAbaby Vista V2?

Newborns can use the Vista V2 from birth with the included bassinet, which provides a flat sleeping surface. Once babies reach 6 months or can sit upright, they transition to the toddler seat.

Is the Vista V2 compatible with car seats?

Yes, the Vista V2 works with most major infant car seats including UPPAbaby Mesa, and adapters are available for other brands like Chicco, Graco, and Maxi-Cosi.

How does the reversible seat compare to other full-size strollers?

The Vista V2's reversible seat is a standout feature allowing seamless parent-facing and forward-facing configurations without removing the seat, offering flexibility competitors rarely match.

What's the weight limit and how long can my child use it?

The Vista V2 supports children up to 50 lbs and typically accommodates kids through age 4-5, making it one of the longest-lasting full-size stroller options available.
