Frequently Asked Questions

Are WiFi baby monitors safe for my baby?

Yes, when used correctly. Choose monitors from reputable brands, use strong passwords, enable two-factor authentication, keep firmware updated, and ensure your home WiFi network is secure.

What features should I look for in a baby monitor with camera?

Essential features include infrared night vision, two-way audio, temperature monitoring, pan/tilt/zoom capability, and reliable connectivity. WiFi models offer remote viewing, while closed-system monitors provide dedicated portability.

Can I use a baby monitor with camera as my baby grows?

Absolutely. Many parents use video monitors beyond infancy to monitor toddlers, check on sleeping children, or supervise play areas. Quality monitors remain useful for several years.

How far away can I view my baby monitor?

Closed-system monitors typically work within 700-1000 feet in open space. WiFi-enabled monitors work anywhere with internet access—including while traveling, at work, or from grandparents' homes.
