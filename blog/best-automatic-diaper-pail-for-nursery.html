Look for pails with adjustable sensor sensitivity. You want it responsive enough to detect hand movement but not so sensitive that it opens at every passing shadow. Read reviews specifically mentioning whether sensors work consistently after 3+ months of use, as some budget models degrade quickly.

Odor Containment System

Motion activation means nothing if smells escape. Check whether the pail uses carbon filters, sealed tube systems, or enzymatic technology. The best starter packs include replacement filters or refill cartridges—budget accordingly. Look for pails that create an air-tight seal between uses, not just when closed.
