Frequently Asked Questions

What age can babies use the Solly Wrap?

The Solly Wrap is designed for use from birth through approximately 35 pounds, making it suitable for newborns and toddlers. Always follow the manufacturer's safety guidelines for proper use.

The Solly Wrap is machine washable on a gentle cycle with cold water. Air drying is recommended to maintain the fabric's integrity and longevity.
