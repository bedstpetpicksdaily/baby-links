Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the Philips Avent Sterilizer take to sterilize bottles?

The sterilization cycle typically takes between 4 to 8 minutes depending on the model and load size. The quick cycle time makes it convenient for busy parents who need sterilized bottles throughout the day.

What items can I sterilize in the Philips Avent Sterilizer?

You can sterilize Philips Avent bottles, nipples, pacifiers, breast pump parts, teethers, and other small feeding accessories. Always check compatibility with non-Avent items before use.

Is the Philips Avent Sterilizer safe for all bottle types?

While designed for Philips Avent bottles, it works with many standard bottle shapes. However, check product specifications as oversized or uniquely shaped bottles may not fit properly.

How often do I need to clean or descale the sterilizer?

Regular descaling every 2-4 weeks is recommended to prevent mineral deposits and maintain optimal performance. Use distilled water when possible to reduce scaling frequency.
