Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Philips Avent Video Baby Monitor WiFi enabled?

No, the Philips Avent operates on a closed digital system between the camera and parent unit. This offers enhanced privacy and security but doesn't include smartphone app connectivity. For WiFi-enabled alternatives, explore other baby monitors on Amazon.

What is the range of the Philips Avent Video Baby Monitor?

The monitor typically offers a range of up to 300 feet in open space, though walls and obstacles may reduce this distance. This makes it ideal for monitoring throughout most homes and even outdoor spaces.

Does the Philips Avent have night vision?

Yes, the camera features infrared night vision that provides clear, detailed images in complete darkness without disturbing your baby with visible light.

Can I use the two-way talk-back feature?

Absolutely. The two-way intercom system allows you to speak to and soothe your baby from anywhere within the monitor's range, making it easier to comfort them without entering the nursery.

How long does the battery last on the parent unit?

Battery life varies based on usage, but most users report 8-12 hours of moderate use before requiring a charge. For extended monitoring sessions, keeping the unit plugged in is recommended.
