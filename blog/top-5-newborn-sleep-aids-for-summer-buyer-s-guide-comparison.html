2. White Noise Machines: Consistent Sound Coverage

Traditional white noise machines remain a popular choice for blocking out summer sounds—from air conditioning units to neighborhood traffic. Premium models offer multiple sound options including rain, ocean waves, and fan noise, allowing customization based on your baby's preferences.

However, while white noise is effective for sound masking, many parents find it less soothing than specialized shushing sounds. Baby Shusher combines the benefits of white noise with the added comfort of a rhythm that actively calms rather than simply masks.

3. Cooling Sleep Sacks: Temperature Management

Summer heat is a legitimate concern for newborn safety. Cooling sleep sacks made from breathable, moisture-wicking fabrics help regulate your baby's body temperature while preventing overheating. Pair these with Baby Shusher for optimal sleep conditions—a cool body combined with soothing sounds creates the perfect sleep environment.

4. Blackout Curtains: Light Control for Better Sleep

Extended summer daylight can confuse newborn circadian rhythms. Quality blackout curtains darken nurseries, signaling bedtime regardless of outside brightness. Combined with a soothing audio aid, they're highly effective for establishing sleep routines.
