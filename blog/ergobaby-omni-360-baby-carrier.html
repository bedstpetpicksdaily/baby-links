Frequently Asked Questions

Can the Ergobaby Omni 360 be used from newborn without inserts?

Yes, the Ergobaby Omni 360 is uniquely designed to accommodate newborns without requiring additional inserts, making it convenient for parents who want an all-in-one solution from day one.

What is the weight limit for the Ergobaby Omni 360?

The carrier is designed for babies from 7 to 45 pounds, supporting children from newborn (with proper positioning) through approximately age 3-4, depending on your child's size.

Is the Ergobaby Omni 360 machine washable?

The carrier has removable, machine-washable fabric components, though the frame and padding should be spot-cleaned only to maintain durability and safety features.

How does the Ergobaby Omni 360 compare to other ergonomic carriers?

While there are other quality ergonomic carriers available, the Omni 360's main advantage is its four built-in positions without additional purchases, superior padding, and proven long-term durability.
