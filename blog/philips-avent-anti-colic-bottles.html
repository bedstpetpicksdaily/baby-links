The patented air vent system separates air from milk, preventing your baby from swallowing air during feeding. This reduces gas buildup, bloating, and the painful symptoms associated with colic, allowing babies to feed more comfortably.

Are Philips Avent bottles compatible with breast pumps?

Yes, Philips Avent Anti-Colic Bottles work directly with Philips Avent breast pumps, allowing seamless transitions between breastfeeding and bottle feeding without requiring additional adapters.
