Finding Your Perfect Fit: Size and Weight Guide

Sleep sack sizing directly impacts effectiveness. Too loose and your baby can wriggle down; too snug and it restricts natural movement and causes overheating. Most experts recommend choosing a size based on your baby's height and weight with minimal extra room. Summer sleep sacks should measure between 0.5 and 1 TOG (thermal overall grade), with many specialists recommending 0.5-0.7 TOG for consistently warm climates.
