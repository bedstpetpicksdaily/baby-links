Understanding White Noise and Baby Sleep

White noise works by providing consistent, neutral sound that masks sudden environmental noises that startle sleeping babies. Unlike silence, which amplifies every creak and outdoor sound, white noise creates an audio buffer that helps babies sleep deeper and longer. This is especially crucial during summer when windows are open and outdoor activity peaks.

Research shows that consistent white noise can reduce the time it takes babies to fall asleep and improve sleep quality by up to 45%. For parents with multiple children, noisy neighborhoods, or those working from home, a dedicated white noise device becomes an essential tool rather than a luxury.

Is white noise safe for newborns?

Yes, white noise is safe for newborns when used correctly. Keep volume at 50-60 decibels (similar to a soft conversation), use timers to prevent continuous overnight exposure, and position devices at least 3 feet from the crib.
