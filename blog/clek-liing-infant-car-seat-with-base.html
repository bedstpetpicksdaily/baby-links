Will this fit in my compact car?

The Liing's footprint is compact, but the real consideration is the base width and how far forward it extends. It fits well in most sedans and crossovers, but tight-fitting compact cars (Honda Fit, Hyundai Venue) can be challenging depending on seat position. Before purchasing, measure your back seat width and the distance from seat to front seat when reclined. The weight advantage matters zero if installation requires an acrobatic feat in your specific vehicle.
