Understanding Baby Sleep Challenges in Hot Weather

Babies struggle to regulate their body temperature, making hot weather particularly problematic for sleep. When rooms are too warm, babies experience increased restlessness, night sweats, and frequent waking. This disruption affects not only their development but also parental sleep quality. White noise machines address this by creating a consistent acoustic environment that helps babies sleep through temperature fluctuations and external noises that become more prevalent during warm months.
