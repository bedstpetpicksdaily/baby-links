The Babysense monitor detects movement cessation and alerts parents, but it's not a SIDS prevention device. It's a monitoring tool that works alongside safe sleep practices like back sleeping and firm mattresses.

Can the Babysense monitor work with any crib?

The monitor is compatible with most standard cribs, portable cribs, and play yards with firm mattresses. Check compatibility with your specific crib model before purchasing.

How long does the battery last?

Battery life varies by model, but most Babysense units provide several days to weeks of operation. The app alerts you when battery is low so you can recharge promptly.
