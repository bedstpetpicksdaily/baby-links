White Noise Science: Why It Works for Baby Sleep

Understanding the science behind Baby Shusher's effectiveness helps parents appreciate the investment. White noise acts as auditory masking, creating a consistent sound environment that filters out disruptive household noises. Research published in pediatric sleep journals shows that babies exposed to consistent white noise fall asleep faster, experience fewer nighttime awakenings, and enjoy longer consolidated sleep periods.
