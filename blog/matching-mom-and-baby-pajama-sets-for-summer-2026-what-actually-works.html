Frequently Asked Questions

Why is bamboo better than cotton for summer baby pajamas?

Bamboo fabric naturally regulates body temperature and wicks moisture, preventing the heavy, sweat-soaked feel cotton develops in heat. Babies overheat easily in summer; bamboo's properties keep them cool without overheating, reducing night sweats and sleep disruption. Bamboo is also hypoallergenic, making it safer for sensitive skin.
