What to Look For

  • Rear-facing capability for at least 2-3 years: Newborns must ride rear-facing, and the safest seats keep children in this position as long as possible. A car seat that's easy to install correctly—whether with LATCH or the vehicle belt—is one you'll actually use properly every single time, which matters more than you'd think.
