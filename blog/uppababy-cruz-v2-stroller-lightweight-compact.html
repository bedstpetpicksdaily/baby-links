How does the Cruz V2 compare to the original Cruz?

Will this work as a primary stroller or only as a travel stroller?

It works as either, but not ideally as only one. The smaller basket and modest recline mean it's limiting for all-day outings with a newborn. As your travel backup or urban primary stroller, it excels. As your only stroller for a family that spends afternoons at parks, you'll feel the limitations.

Is the compact fold worth the tradeoff in recline and basket space?

Depends entirely on your life. If you fly twice a year or park in tight spots, yes. If you never travel and have a garage, the recline and basket matter more than the compact fold. Honest answer: test whether your actual problems get solved before committing to the price.
