Why Bamboo Pajamas Are Perfect for Summer Babies

Bamboo fabric is naturally thermoregulating, making it an excellent choice for newborns during hot months. Unlike traditional materials that trap heat and moisture, bamboo breathes exceptionally well, allowing air to circulate around your baby's skin. This natural property helps prevent heat rash, night sweats, and the discomfort that often leads to disrupted sleep. Additionally, bamboo is hypoallergenic and gentle on sensitive newborn skin, making it ideal for babies prone to eczema or other skin sensitivities.
