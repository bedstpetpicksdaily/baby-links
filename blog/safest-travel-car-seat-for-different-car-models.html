Test the installation in each of your vehicles before purchasing to confirm secure fit.
  • Compact Design with Wide Base: Look for car seats with a narrower profile that fits comfortably between bucket seats in smaller vehicles, while still maintaining a wide, stable base for safety.   • Adjustable Features Across Growth Stages: Select seats with multiple harness positions and adjustable headrests that grow with your child from newborn to toddler. This flexibility means you won't need to replace the seat as your child grows, and adjustable features help accommodate different vehicle seat angles and heights.
