Will it actually help my baby's reflux?

Probably somewhat, based on parent feedback. The inclined design helps gravity work in your favor during and after feeding, which can reduce spit-up. Talk to your pediatrician about your baby's specific reflux before treating this as a solution.

It's reasonable to expect 1-2 years of solid performance before it starts flattening, which is standard for foam-based baby gear in this price range.
