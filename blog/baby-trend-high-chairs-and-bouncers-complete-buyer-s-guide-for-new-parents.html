Frequently Asked Questions

At what age can my baby use a Baby Trend high chair?

Most Baby Trend high chairs are designed for babies 6 months and older who can sit up independently. Always check your specific model's age and weight recommendations before use.

Are Baby Trend bouncers safe for newborns?

Yes, many Baby Trend bouncers accommodate newborns with appropriate recline positions and support. However, never leave a baby unattended in any bouncer, and follow all safety guidelines in the product manual.

How do I clean a Baby Trend high chair?

Most Baby Trend high chairs feature removable, machine-washable seat covers and wipeable trays. Simply remove the fabric components and wipe down hard surfaces with a damp cloth for quick cleanups.

Can Baby Trend bouncers be used for extended periods?

While comfortable for play and soothing, bouncers shouldn't be used as a sleeping place or left unattended for extended periods. Use them as part of a balanced routine with supervised tummy time and other activities.
