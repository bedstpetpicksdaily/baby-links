Frequently Asked Questions

How long can my child realistically stay in the harness mode?

Most kids max out the harness around age 5-6 or 50 pounds, whichever comes first. The Joie maxes at 25 kg (55 lbs), so you get about 4-5 solid years of harness use. After that, the backless booster mode keeps them safe-belted until roughly age 8-10 depending on their height.

Does it work with ISOFIX in most cars?

The Joie Elevate uses ISOFIX connectors (called LATCH in the US), and they're standard-strength—not the reinforced type. This works in nearly every 2015+ vehicle, but always verify your car's manual. The seat installs securely, though the connectors can feel slightly loose if you're expecting maximum rigidity.

Is this seat safe in a three-across setup?

Yes, but barely. At under 7 pounds, the Elevate is narrow enough to fit three across in most sedans, though legroom becomes tight for passengers. In minivans or larger SUVs, three car seats across is comfortable. The padding doesn't add bulk the way some competitors do, which actually helps here.
