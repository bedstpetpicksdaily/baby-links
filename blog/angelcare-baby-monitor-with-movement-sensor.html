Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Angelcare movement sensor work?

The sensor pad is placed under the mattress and detects even the smallest movements from your baby. If no movement is detected within a set timeframe, an alarm alerts you on the parent unit, providing an extra layer of safety beyond video monitoring.

Is the Angelcare monitor secure and encrypted?

Yes, Angelcare uses encrypted signals between the camera and parent unit to protect your privacy and prevent unauthorized access to your baby's room feed.

Can I use the Angelcare monitor with a play yard or pack-and-play?

The movement sensor pad is designed for standard crib mattresses. Using it with play yards or pack-and-plays may be challenging due to the thinner mattress design, so check compatibility before purchasing.

Does Angelcare offer smartphone app monitoring?

Yes, newer Angelcare models include smartphone app integration, allowing you to monitor your baby from anywhere with an internet connection through your mobile device.
