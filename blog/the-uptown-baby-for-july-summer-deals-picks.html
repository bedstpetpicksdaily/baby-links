What's the difference between a sleep sack and a swaddle?

Sleep sacks are wearable blankets that provide safety and warmth without loose bedding, ideal for older babies and toddlers. Swaddles are designed to snugly wrap newborns, promoting comfort and the startle reflex reduction. The Uptown Baby offers both options.
