What to Look For

  • Anti-colic and venting systems: Babies with sensitive stomachs are prone to gas, bloating, and discomfort. Look for bottles with advanced venting technology or anti-colic valves that minimize air intake during feeding.   • Hypoallergenic, BPA-free materials: Sensitive-stomach babies often react to certain plastics and additives.
