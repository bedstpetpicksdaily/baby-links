Summer brings unique challenges for baby sleep: longer daylight hours, warmer temperatures, and increased household activity can all disrupt a baby's rest. Quality soothing devices help create a consistent sleep environment regardless of external conditions. Investing in the right soothing device can prevent sleep regression and establish healthy sleep habits early on.
