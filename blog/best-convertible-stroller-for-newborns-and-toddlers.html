  • Reversible Seat Configuration: The ability to turn the seat around to face you during those early months—and then face forward when your toddler becomes more interested in the world—is invaluable.   • Substantial Weight Capacity and Durability: Choose a stroller built to handle the transition from a 7-pound newborn to a 35+ pound toddler, with a frame that won't feel wobbly or worn out by year three. Quality materials and solid construction ensure your investment actually lasts through multiple children if needed.
