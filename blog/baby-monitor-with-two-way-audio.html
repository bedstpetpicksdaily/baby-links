Frequently Asked Questions

What is two-way audio on a baby monitor?

Two-way audio allows you to hear your baby and speak back to them through the monitor. Unlike one-way audio monitors, this feature lets your baby hear your voice, which can help soothe them when you're not in the room.

Do I need WiFi for a baby monitor with two-way audio?

Not all two-way audio monitors require WiFi. Some use closed digital signals with a parent unit, while others connect via WiFi for remote smartphone access. Choose based on your preference for range and convenience.

How far can a baby monitor with two-way audio reach?

Range varies by model. Closed-system monitors typically reach 700-900 feet indoors, while WiFi-enabled monitors work anywhere you have internet connectivity, including outside your home.

Are two-way audio baby monitors safe?

Yes, when purchased from reputable brands. Look for monitors with secure encryption, two-factor authentication, and regular security updates to protect your baby's privacy and your home's security.
