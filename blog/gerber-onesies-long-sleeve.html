Frequently Asked Questions

Are Gerber long sleeve onesies machine washable?

Yes, Gerber onesies are machine washable in cold water and tumble dry on low heat. Avoid fabric softeners to maintain softness and durability.

What size should I buy for my newborn?

Newborns typically wear newborn and 0-3 month sizes. Gerber runs slightly small, so consider sizing up for longer wear and comfort.

Do Gerber onesies shrink after washing?

Minimal shrinkage occurs with proper cold water washing and low-heat drying. Following care instructions helps maintain original sizing.

Are these onesies suitable for sensitive skin?

Gerber's cotton-blend fabric is gentle on most babies, though always patch test if your baby has diagnosed skin sensitivities or eczema.
