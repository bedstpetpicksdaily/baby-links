What age can babies use the SwaddleMe swaddle wrap?

The SwaddleMe is suitable for newborns from birth through approximately 6 months, depending on baby's size. Always follow weight and length guidelines provided by the manufacturer.

Is the SwaddleMe swaddle wrap safe for co-sleeping?

The SwaddleMe wrap is designed for crib use. It's best to follow safe sleep guidelines and place your swaddled baby on their back in a firm, flat sleep surface without loose bedding.
