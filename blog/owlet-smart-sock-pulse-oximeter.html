Yes, the Owlet Smart Sock+ is designed for infants from newborn to 18 months. The sock uses medical-grade pulse oximetry and has been tested for safety. However, consult your pediatrician if your baby has specific health conditions.

The Owlet Smart Sock+ battery typically lasts 24 hours per charge. Most parents charge it overnight alongside their baby's sleep schedule for continuous monitoring.
