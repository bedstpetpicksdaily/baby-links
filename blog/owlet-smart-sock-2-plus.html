Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Owlet Smart Sock 2 Plus safe for newborns?

Yes, the device is FDA-cleared and designed for newborns. The soft sock is adjustable and comfortable for infants from birth through 18 months old.

How accurate are the heart rate and oxygen readings?

The Owlet uses pulse oximetry technology similar to hospital-grade monitors. While generally accurate, readings can occasionally be affected by sock fit and movement.

What happens if oxygen levels drop?

You'll receive instant notifications on your smartphone if heart rate or oxygen levels fall below preset thresholds, allowing immediate response.

How long does the battery last?

The Smart Sock 2 Plus battery typically lasts 16-24 hours between charges, depending on usage and monitoring frequency.
