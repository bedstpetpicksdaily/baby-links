Frequently Asked Questions

Are blackout curtains safe for babies?

Yes, quality blackout curtains designed for nurseries are safe for babies. Choose cordless options or keep cords secured and out of reach to prevent tangling hazards. Always verify products meet CPSC safety standards.

How much darkness do blackout curtains provide?

Most blackout curtains block 95-99% of light. For best results, ensure they cover the entire window and extend beyond the frame to prevent light from entering around the edges.

Can blackout curtains help with temperature control?

Yes, blackout curtains provide insulation by blocking heat in summer and trapping warmth in winter, helping maintain a consistent, comfortable nursery temperature.

What size blackout curtains do I need?

Measure your window width and height, then select curtains that extend at least 4-8 inches beyond the window frame on all sides for maximum light blocking effectiveness.
